Computer Engineering at Queen's University

Information about the course

In Queen's university's computer engineering program, students learn about many aspects related to the computers, like hardware, software and communication courses.Hardware area covers computer architecture, organization and circuit engineering. Software area covers operating systems, programming languages software design and many other things related to software engineering. Communication courses go over wireless networking, protocols and information transfer. Computer engineering is a part of Electrical and Computer Engineering Faculty (ECE).

About Queen's university

Queen's University in Kingston has been operating for more than 175 years after being established by the Royal Charter of Queen Victoria.It currently has more than 24,000 students. Along with computer engineering, Queen's offers many other courses including many other forms of engineering, sciences, cancer research, social sciences and many more, offering a total of 89 programs.
