Computer Engineering at Queen's University

Requirements

These are the following requirements that need to be met in order to be accepted to this course.

  • Ontario Secondary School Diploma
  • Grade 12 English
  • Grade 12 Chemistry
  • Grade 12 Physics
  • Grade 12 Calculus and Vectors

One of the following Grade 12 courses:

  • Advanced Functions
  • Mathematics of Data Management
  • Biology
  • Computer Science or Earth and Space Science
  • One additional Grade 12 university or university/college course
